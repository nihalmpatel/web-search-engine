Description

Discover the weather, plan your day and stay informed! Simple, beautiful & accurate. Get detailed info and behold the current weather visualized with stunning weather videos. Check your city and multiple locations worldwide. Experience the weather with Weather+.

WHY YOU’LL LOVE WEATHER+
• Forecast Daily & Hourly
• Humidity, Precipitation, Pressure & Wind direction
• Speed & Visibility details
• Personalize your Forecast Layout
• Visualized with realistic weather videos
• Animated Flip Clock
• Dark Mode
• Split View for multitasking support
• Full Apple Watch support

DARK MODE

FindingANameTakesTooMuchTime , 10/08/2017

My GO-TO-WEATHER APP!

Always has humidity! This seems to have a major effect on my health so I care about that whether it’s cool or hot. Simple, easy (quick) to read with info I can use, not like weathermaps and radar. What do I care about that? It’s the weatherman’s job to tell me whats to expect, at least in the next 24 hours, which is the most an individual may have to adapt to any weather since it is simply too changeable. Oh, and the Fahrenheit-Celsius option is spot on as I have Family in three different countries and I like to know how they’re coping with climate change. An app well-done!

First1Man , 12/05/2017

Clean & Simple. Expensive!

Very nice app. Straight forward info. Quick to load. Current data appears correct based on other app info. Will keep around for a few days to see if I still like it. The reasons for 3 stars: Like it as I may, could NEVER consider your subscription offer. 12 month subscription for $8.50 - just for simple data I can get through dozens of apps. Plus, there’s no imaging maps - no radar, no satellite...if you offered those and it was good implementation I’d consider your price as a one time fee. I’m with other reviewer that the clock takes up too much space. I’d rather see current temp and wind speed in that size. Also, the graphic could be a bit more elegant than two blocks. Working with it for a few days will probably bring up other thoughts. Pop ups are the worst ads to me. Not enticing in the least and makes me (and other of my friends) irritated. NOT the right feeling to get people in the mood to LIKE your product. Hope you find a happy medium with your pricing. Cheers.

PumaPines , 08/30/2017

Beautiful, Accurate, Concise

Terrific weather scenery behind a concise weather & time display. Weather videos show a variety of environments (mountains, tropical, forest, grasslands) and reflect the current weather. The only thing that I would suggest would be a live tile and/or widget to reflect the current time. The app tile now shows a clock... but it's not the current time. It would be great if that could be made active. Otherwise a great app! Thank you!
