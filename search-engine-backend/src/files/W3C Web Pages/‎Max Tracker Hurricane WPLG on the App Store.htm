Description

The Max Tracker Hurricane app is powered by Max Mayfield, former director of the National Hurricane Center, Meteorologists Bryan Norcross and WPLG’s Chief Certified Meteorologist Betty Davis. This team of meteorologists brings you the latest and most accurate information on developing tropical systems to help keep you and your family safe.

• Projected Hurricane Paths
• Interactive Live Storm Tracker
• Local Radar
• Live Local 10 news storm coverage
• Severe Weather Alerts
• Tropical Weather Outlooks
• Watches and Warnings

cheme75 , 09/26/2017

Essential app for hurricane season

Great app to keep up with storm tracking... only gripe for me is too many notifications. Asking for local storms only does not seem to limit notifications for storms that have moved hundreds of miles away. I like the notifications for approaching storms but why the constant stream of continuing notifications for storms moved hundreds of miles away, some a thousand miles from us and dying out. It's a small issue but aggravating when it fills the notification list with useless notices for storms that passed by days ago. Let me know if it turns back for some strange reason, otherwise end the notifications when it passes. Otherwise I'd consider it an essential app for us in SoFlo. The plan and prepare section with contacts for shelters, govt assistance numbers, etc is also essential.
