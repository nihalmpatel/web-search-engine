Description

Weather: Prepare for your day with accurate hourly, 5-day forecasts

Weather: A useful & needful weather app for you.

Weather provides accurate real-time weather, hourly & daily weather forecast and weather details, is your best choice to get weather information.

Thiedea , 07/08/2018

Much to like!

I was apprehensive about downloading this app but after several months I’m ready to say I like it! Love the messages in the morning and evening about what the local weather will be. And it’s accurate, a challenge on coastal California. I like the ease of checking out weather in other cities. I’d like it better when I put in a city that the state pulls up automatically so I can choose the one I want, but the state doesn’t always show. I also like the 10 day forecast. There’s more to learn with this app so perhaps I’ll update the review in a few months.

vtspnr , 01/29/2019

Not great

I haven’t yet found a weather app that combines at least near-accuracy with usefulness, but after trying this one for several months, this isn’t it. Today’s weather is often yesterday’s, claiming to be today )it’s Tuesday now, but it shows Monday as today). The mini summary (such as ‘cloudy with a chance of...’) is cut off after just a few letters, with no way to click on it to actually read it. Its summary descriptions vary unaccountably (-5 is ‘frigid’ sometimes, merely ‘very cold’ others). The hourly forecast for Today is good, and very useful (especially when Today actually IS today), but that feature isn’t available for even one day in the future.
So I guess I’ll keep looking for a weather app...
One more complaint: finding a nickname that hasn’t been chosen already is extraordinarily difficult (I’ve just tried about 20, including in foreign languages!), leaving me to wonder whether the developer really wants reviews...

IntiBLUE , 04/28/2018

Solid, Reliable Software

I changed apps when the apple Weather app started loading without current temperature or forecasts. I tried a couple other apps, but this one is simple and solid. Adds are unobtrusive if they are there, and I’ve had no loading problems.

I generally use the hour-to-hour and 5 day forecast. Weather being what it is in PNW, it’s not perfectly accurate. Honestly I’d be freaked out if it was always right! Weather prediction is still an art *and* science profession.
