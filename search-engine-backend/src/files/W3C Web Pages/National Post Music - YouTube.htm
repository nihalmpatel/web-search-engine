  • National Post Music: Channel Trailer

    4,750 views 2 years ago
    Weekly, NP Music hosts musicians from all over the world for performances of original songs and covers. Music fans can check here weekly for critically acclaimed names in music, including pop stars, Indie rock icons, soul singers, Hollywood stars, music legends and many more. Unplugged or plugged in, NP Music welcomes acts from all genres. Guests have included The Wanted, Abdominal, Great Big World, GlasVegas, The Trews, Bry Webb, The Darcys, Matt Andersen, Born Ruffians, The Shoe, Basia Bulat, Rise Against, First Aid Kit, Jenny Lewis, The Arkells, Bahamas, Coldspecks, Zeus, Hanson, Robyn Hitchcock, Paolo Nutini, Lights, Meghan Trainor, Hayden, The Tea Party, Alan Doyle, Ron Sexsmith, Nate Mendel, Apocalyptica, Wintersleep, The Dandy Warhols, Jimmy Eat World and more.
