Description

Create and discover the best mobile Stories and digital content right on the Issuu App. The Issuu Story Cloud offers a suite of publishing tools to create Stories and full-length publications for social and mobile in an immersive reading experience.

CREATE STORIES
Issuu is the storytelling platform and the first-ever integrated Issuu Story Cloud for creators, marketers, editors and businesses. Now you can create mobile Stories on the Issuu app straight from your uploaded content. With just a few clicks you can build, preview and share Stories on Instagram and publish in the AMP format to broaden your audience and tap into the power of mobile Stories—today’s hottest content format. Download the Issuu App to create Stories or read Stories.

Issuu Story Cloud tools include:
Share Stories to Instagram
Select Story Style
Build Stories from text + photo content
Preview Visual Stories in app
Add animations
Generate a video for each page of content for easy sharing on your social networks
Publish in AMP format on amp.issuu.com
Save videos to phone

DISCOVER THE BEST STORYTELLING
Discover, read and share the most exciting and unique storytelling available. Read Stories and full-length magazines, catalogs, ebooks, and more from major brands, independent publishers and the next generation of creators in fashion, music, art and more on Issuu.

Cajun Bella , 07/28/2019

Altruistic is Not Issuu

I’m discouraged by Issuu’s unwillingness to provide non-profits with an affordable option to publish all of their life-changing stories so worthy of being told. Most technology software companies are happy to do so, because it’s technology and doesn’t affect the company’s bottom line. Supporting non-profits in that way is their way of giving back to the community while also helping the non-profit connect to the people most passionate about their work. At $35 per month with the annual subscription discount, there are too many children to feed, debilitating diseases to cure, homeless veterans to shelter, etc. for a non-profit to conscientiously put $35 of their donor’s dollars each month into the hands of Issuu. Issuu, a company that thinks that annual subscription discount and charging for features of convenience (rather than performance) should suffice for non-profit needs. They feel so strongly about not supporting non-profits, they had to make a FAQ saying No we don’t provide a discount. I’d look into Salesforce’s charitable giving model. I don’t think it’s a coincidence why they, Facebook or Google have had such great success. Charitable causes connects people, creates respect, affinity and trust. Thanks anyway Issuu. Good luck to you!
