Postmedia Network Inc. is the largest publisher by circulation of paid English-language daily newspapers in Canada, representing some of the country's best known media brands and digital properties including The Vancouver Sun, The Province, Calgary Herald, Edmonton Journal, Saskatoon StarPhoenix, Regina Leader-Post, National Post, Windsor Star, Ottawa Citizen and Montreal Gazette. Reaching millions of Canadians every week, Postmedia Network engages readers and offers advertisers and marketers integrated solutions to effectively reach target audiences through a variety of print, online, digital, and mobile platforms.

Postmedia welcomes your partnership application and is proud to give back to the communities in which we live. Your request can be for a national partnership with Postmedia, with selected multiple markets or with one specific market and may be accompanied by documents such as sponsorship packages and photos.

Your request will be reviewed by our sponsorship team and you will receive feedback in a minimum of four weeks. Please note that Postmedia does not provide cash sponsorships, projects which are personal, religious or political in nature. A request made less than four (4) weeks before the start of an event or project will be declined. As such, requests should be made well in advance of the start date of the project.
