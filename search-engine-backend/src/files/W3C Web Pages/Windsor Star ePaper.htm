Take a look at our features

Our ePapers are full of interactive features to engage and delight news lovers. Each publication includes several features that enhance your reading and sharing experience.

DOWNLOAD

Download your ePaper on your tablet, smartphone or laptop, and read it anywhere.

OFFLINE

Once your ePaper is downloaded you don't need wi-fi. You can read it anywhere, even on your commute.

LISTEN

You can even have articles read out to you while you’re busy doing other things.

COMMENT

Post your comments on articles you agree or disagree with.

SHARE

Share articles or images you enjoy with family and friends.

ALERT

Receive an email alert as soon as the current issue of the paper is available. Activate under "My Services" » "My Publications".

BACK ISSUES

Remember that great article you read yesterday but forgot to bookmark? You can still read it through the calendar located above your publication.

SUPPLEMENTS

Wondering where your newspaper supplements are? Click the button ≡ followed by Publications to see a list of all available supplements.

PLATFORM-WIDE

Want to access your favourite newspaper/magazine on your mobile device? Download the app and log in with your credentials.
