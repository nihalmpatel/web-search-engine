Share Adjust Comment Print Business Improvement Areas (BIA), municipalities and other organizations in Windsor-Essex have been partnering with the Windsor Star to promote themselves for years for one reason. It works. Among the specialty products that the Star produces, through its Custom Content team, are small format glossy magazines. These handy, useful products stay in readers’ homes long after they’re published and serve as guides to events, maps, promotions, profiles and more. To check out some of publications, click on this link. For an organization that represents its members in a geographical area, like a BIA, or with common interests and goals, our magazines are a great way to connect with readers.
