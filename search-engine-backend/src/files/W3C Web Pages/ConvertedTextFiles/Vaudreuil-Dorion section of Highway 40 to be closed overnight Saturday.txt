John Mahoney / Montreal Gazette Share Adjust Comment Print Highway 40 in Vaudreuil-Dorion will be completely closed to traffic starting Saturday night as demolition work is carried out on the Chemin des Chenaux overpass. The shutdown will begin at 8:30 p.m. Saturday and the highway is scheduled to re-open at 10:30 a.m. Sunday. The demolition was deemed necessary after an inspection of the overpass in September 2018 by Transport Quebec found structural defects that called its safety into question.
