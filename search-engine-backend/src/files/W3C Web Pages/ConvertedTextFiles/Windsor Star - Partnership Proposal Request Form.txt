Reaching millions of Canadians every week, Postmedia Network engages readers and offers advertisers and marketers integrated solutions to effectively reach target audiences through a variety of print, online, digital, and mobile platforms. Postmedia welcomes your partnership application and is proud to give back to the communities in which we live. Your request can be for a national partnership with Postmedia, with selected multiple markets or with one specific market and may be accompanied by documents such as sponsorship packages and photos. Your request will be reviewed by our sponsorship team and you will receive feedback in a minimum of four weeks. Please note that Postmedia does not provide cash sponsorships, projects which are personal, religious or political in nature. A request made less than four (4) weeks before the start of an event or project will be declined. As such, requests should be made well in advance of the start date of the project.   Organization Name * Address * City * Province Province: Alberta British Columbia Manitoba New Brunswick Newfoundland and Labrador Northwest Territories Nova Scotia Nunavut Ontario Prince Edward Island Quebec Saskatchewan Yukon * Postal Code * Website * Contact Name * Title * Telephone Number * Email Address * Program/Event Description * Program/Event Date(s): Example: (mm/dd/yyyy) * Purpose Statement * Who are you trying to reach? * Total expected attendance * Total amount of advertising budget committed to Postmedia (print, digital and printing) * Please list all partnership benefits/visibility for Postmedia and assign values to each benefit * Other Sponsors * File Upload (File is limited to 2 MB) All personal information is treated in accordance with our Privacy Statement.
