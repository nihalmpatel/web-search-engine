James Wells / The Beacon Herald Share Adjust Comment Print A 37-year-old Windsor woman died in a crash off Highway 401 in Chatham-Kent early Sunday morning. Ontario Provincial Police said in a news release Sunday that a motor vehicle travelling westbound on Highway 401 near Orford Road in Chatham-Kent left the roadway and came to a stop in a treed area on the north side of the highway. The OPP, Chatham-Kent Emergency Medical Services and Chatham-Kent Fire Department responded to the single motor vehicle collision at about 1 a.m. Sunday. The female driver was the only person in the vehicle and was pronounced dead at the scene.
