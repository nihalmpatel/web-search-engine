Join the Issuu Reseller Program to earn commissions and help your customers build their brands through their digital content. INQUIRE ABOUT PARTNERING As a Reseller Partner, you refer or resell brands to join Issuu as paid publishers. In return, you can earn a 20% commission on average for each Premium or Optimum plan you help sign up. Your brand also gets featured on Issuu’s Reseller Partner Page, showcasing your company’s core competencies.  Why Issuu? Our Reseller Program offers creative agencies, technology partners and resellers the opportunity to grow your revenue, expand your business and help your customers build their brands by sharing, measuring and monetizing their digital content. We offer everything you need for your digital content to be successful on the Issuu Story Cloud: A seamless reading experience, multiple ways to share, reader statistics, ability to sell and global brand awareness are all available to our content creators. Our network consists of affiliates who align themselves with the world leader of digital publishing, reaping the benefits of helping their customers develop global brand awareness through their digital content.
